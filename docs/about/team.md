# Team & Philosophy

We are a small team of cognitive infrastructure architects.

## How we scale

Not by volume—by rigor:

- repeatable methodology
- durable domain models
- enforceable governance

## What we avoid

- prompt-only safety
- model worship
- fragile demos that can’t survive audits
