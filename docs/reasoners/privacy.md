## 3) What data we process

<div class="landing-section">
	<div class="landing-card">
		<ul>
			<li><strong>Contact and inquiry data</strong>: information you submit when contacting us (e.g., name, email, message content).</li>
			<li><strong>Usage data</strong>: aggregated information about how visitors use the Site (pages viewed, time on page, referrer, approximate location).</li>
			<li><strong>Technical data</strong>: device/browser details, anonymized or truncated IP information where supported, and diagnostics needed to operate the Site securely.</li>
		</ul>
		<p class="landing-mini">
			We do not intentionally collect special categories of personal data.
			Please do not include sensitive information in messages unless necessary.
		</p>
	</div>
</div>

<div class="landing-section">
